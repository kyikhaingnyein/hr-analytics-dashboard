# HR Analytics Dashboard

**1. HR Report: Headcount Analysis**
The organization maintains a total headcount of 3,409 employees (as of the 2012–2023 reporting period).

Departmental & Level Distribution
Largest Departments: Software (660) and Sales (510) are the primary drivers of headcount, followed by Marketing and HR.
Organizational Hierarchy: The vast majority of the workforce consists of Individual Contributors (2,551), followed by Team Leads (493) and Management (279).

Demographics & Education
Gender: The workforce is majority male (61%) vs. female (39%).
Age: The distribution peaks between the ages of 30 and 45, suggesting a mid-career heavy workforce.
Education: A high academic standard  is maintained, with 2,686 employees holding Bachelor's degrees and 527 holding Master's degrees.
Marital Status: 71% of the workforce is single, which may correlate with the high percentage of individual contributors and the peak age range.

Location & Work Mode
Work Style: 80% of employees work Remote, wiht only 20% On-site.
Top Cities: San Francisco (408) and Los Angeles (341) are the primary hubs.

**2. HR Report: Employee Turnover Analysis**
The overall turnover rate is high at 43%, indicating significant challenges in talent retention.
